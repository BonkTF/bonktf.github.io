Coverage


We supply the broadband service connected via Customer Premise Equipment (CPE) - which is usually on the outside of your premises - and from there via an ethernet cable to a power injector - a small (3" x 1.5" x 1") black device connected to your electricity mains - and finally an ethernet cable which you can plug into a router, your computer or a switch. We need to have a line of sight from somewhere on your property to one of our access points to connect you.
